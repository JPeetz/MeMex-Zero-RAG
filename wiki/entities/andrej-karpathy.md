# Andrej Karpathy

AI researcher and engineer known for foundational work in deep learning, computer vision, and making AI accessible through education.

## Background

- Co-founder of OpenAI
- Former Director of AI at Tesla (Autopilot)
- PhD from Stanford (computer vision)
- Creator of popular AI educational content

[Source: wiki/sources/karpathy-llm-wiki-gist.md]

## Contributions

### LLM Wiki Pattern (2026)

Published a GitHub gist describing a pattern for LLM-maintained personal knowledge bases. The key insight: compile knowledge into a wiki instead of retrieving chunks (RAG). [Source: wiki/sources/karpathy-llm-wiki-gist.md]

### Educational Impact

- Neural Networks: Zero to Hero (YouTube series)
- minGPT, nanoGPT implementations
- Made transformer architecture accessible

## Notable Quote

> "The human's job is to curate sources, direct the analysis, ask good questions, and think about what it all means. The LLM's job is everything else."
