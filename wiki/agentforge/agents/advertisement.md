## Pipeline

```
DIAGNOSE → RESEARCH → STRATEGIZE → CREATE → OPTIMIZE → QC → EXPORT
```

**1. DIAGNOSE:** Define product, category, offer, audience, conversion barrier, emotional lever, viral angle.
**2. RESEARCH:** Web research for: viral formats, competitor ads, audience intelligence, meme/creator patterns, search intent, seasonal context. 9 sub-categories of research covered.
**3. STRATEGIZE:** Big idea, primary promise, secondary hooks, proof devices, audience-segment matching.
**4. CREATE:** Platform-specific ad concepts for X (Tweet + Thread), TikTok (Trend-jacking + Founder + UGC), Instagram (Reel + Carousel + Story). Image prompts, video scripts, storyboards, shot lists.
**5. OPTIMIZE:** SEO (keywords, meta, alt text, FAQ, semantic coverage) + GEO (query headings, answer-first, fact clusters, entity clarity, low-ambiguity language).
**6. QC:** Claims verification, brand voice consistency, platform fit, distinctness check, vague language removal.
**7. EXPORT:** Structured, copyable campaign document → MeMex + Obsidian.

## Creative Standard (Per Campaign)
- 1 big idea
- 3-7 creative angles
- 5+ hooks
- 3+ image concepts (with AI generation prompts, composition, lighting, color mood)
- 3+ video concepts (with storyboards, shot lists, timed scripts, edit style notes)
- 3+ short-form scripts
- 3+ caption options per platform
- 5+ CTA variants
- Proof-led, curiosity-led, founder/UGC, direct-response, and trend-native concepts all required
- Low-friction + high-conviction versions

## Research Coverage (9 Categories)
1. Viral formats on TikTok, Instagram Reels, X
2. Trending creative patterns in product category
3. Competitor ad angles and messaging
4. User pain points, objections, desire signals, social proof
5. Meme structures, creator-style content, short-form hook styles
6. Platform-specific posting patterns and engagement mechanics
7. Search intent and informational language for SEO
8. AI answer-engine discoverability for GEO
9. Seasonal, cultural, and news-adjacent relevance

## Platform Rules
- **X:** Sharp hooks, contrarian framing, thread-ready, quote-tweetability
- **TikTok:** Native creator voice, first 2 seconds critical, cuts every 2-3s, 15-45s
- **Instagram:** Polished but native Reels, carousel narratives, story-driven, caption hierarchy

## Compliance
- No invented claims, stats, endorsements, or guarantees
- Unverified claims flagged and rewritten to safe versions
- Regulated categories flagged
- No misleading framing, deceptive urgency, false scarcity, dark patterns
