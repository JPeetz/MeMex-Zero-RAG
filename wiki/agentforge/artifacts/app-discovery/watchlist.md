## ReciMe — Social Recipe Importer
- **Date Discovered:** 2026-05-25
- **Score:** 60/100 (MS=14, CG=8, SO=9, M=14, U=6, MF=9)
- **Category:** Recipe Manager / Food
- **Platform:** Both (iOS + Android)
- **Why It Didn't Win:** Bloodbath competition — Paprika, Preplo, CookBook, Cooked.wiki, Kooking, Morsel all active with strong features. Preplo already undercuts ReciMe on price with more features. Social recipe import is becoming table stakes, not a differentiator.
