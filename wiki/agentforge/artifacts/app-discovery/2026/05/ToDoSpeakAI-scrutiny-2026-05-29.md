# To-Do Speak AI — Competitor Scrutiny Report

**Date:** 2026-05-29 | **Pipeline:** App Discovery v4.0 | **Scaffold Winner:** ThoughtLatch

---

## Section 1: Product Overview

- **Name:** To-Do Speak AI: Voice Tasks
- **Platform:** iOS only
- **Status:** Live on iOS App Store (ID: 6755624768)
- **Category:** Productivity / Task Management
- **Price:** Free + In-App Purchases (pricing unlisted)
- **Developer:** Individual/small team, no visible company entity

To-Do Speak AI positions itself as a simple voice-to-task converter: "Speak your tasks and let To-Do Speak AI turn them into a smart to-do list. Stay hands-free, organized, and never forget an important task again."

---

## Section 2: Feature Deep-Dive

**Advertised Features:**
- Voice-to-task conversion: Speak → task list created
- Smart categorization (basic AI sorting)
- Reminders: Standard iOS notification reminders
- Hands-free operation: Core use case is driving/cooking/occupied-hands scenarios

**Missing Features (vs ThoughtLatch):**
- No ADHD-specific positioning or UX design
- No on-device AI processing (likely cloud-based given "AI" branding)
- No voice tone/urgency detection
- No calendar sync
- No widget
- No thought stream or capture-for-later paradigm — it's purely "speak a task, get a task"
- No streak or gamification
- No data export
- No Android version
- No Siri/App Intents integration
- No shareable milestone cards

**App Store Listing Quality:** Basic. Screenshots show functional UI but no emotional hook or ADHD positioning. Generic "voice to-do" approach competes with Siri Reminders, not with purpose-built ADHD tools.

---

## Section 3: Competitive Position

**Strengths:**
1. **Live on App Store** — available for download, passing App Store review
2. **Clean name** — "To-Do Speak AI" is keyword-rich, likely ranking for "voice to-do" and "speak AI tasks"
3. **Simple value prop** — "Speak your tasks" is easy to understand

**Weaknesses:**
1. **Zero traction** — App Store shows "Not enough ratings to display" (fewer than 5 reviews)
2. **No ADHD focus** — generic productivity positioning misses the highest-intent audience
3. **No AI moat** — "AI" in the name but no evidence of on-device processing, personalization, or intelligent features beyond basic categorization
4. **No differentiation from Siri** — "Hey Siri, remind me to..." already does 80% of what this app does
5. **iOS only** — no Android presence
6. **Unknown developer** — no brand, no community, no trust signals
7. **Pricing invisible** — users can't see IAP prices before downloading, which reduces conversion

## Section 4: Market Position Analysis

**App Store Traction:** Minimal. "Not enough ratings." App ID 6755624768 suggests late-2024 or 2025 launch. No visible growth.

**Threat Level to ThoughtLatch: LOW**
- Zero user base. Zero reviews. Zero brand recognition.
- Generic positioning does not compete for ADHD users
- Siri Reminders is their real competitor, not ThoughtLatch
- The app has been live for months with no traction — it's essentially dead on arrival

**What Would Change Threat Level:**
- Pivot to ADHD-specific positioning with UX redesign
- Acquisition by a larger productivity company
- Cross-platform launch with AI differentiation
- App Store featuring or editorial placement
