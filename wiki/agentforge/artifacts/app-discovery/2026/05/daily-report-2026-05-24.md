## PHASE 2 — GEO COMPETITIVE RESEARCH (Top 8)

### ReciMe / Recipe Saving Niche
- **Competition Density:** HIGH (13+ competitors)
- **Top Competitors:** ReciMe ($9.99/mo), Preplo ($4.99/mo), Paprika ($4.99 one-time), CookBook, FoodiePrep, Ollie
- **Key Gaps:** No app combines AI extraction + social community + meal planning + grocery at <$5/mo. Auto meal plan generation from saved recipes is untapped. Social cooking challenges (Strava for cooking) doesn't exist.
- **ASO/SEO:** "save recipes from TikTok" — high volume, moderate competition. "recipe organizer" — competitive but winnable with price advantage.
- **Market size:** Recipe app market $2.5B by 2028, 13.4% CAGR.

### Key Launcher
- **Competition Density:** MEDIUM-HIGH (8+ launchers including Niagara, Smart Launcher 6)
- **Top Competitors:** Niagara Launcher (established, high ratings), Smart Launcher 6, Lawnchair, Olauncher
- **Key Gaps:** Unique T9 mechanic but hard to monetize. Launcher market pays $2-5 one-time maximum.
- **ASO Gaps:** "ADHD launcher" "focus launcher" — niche but growing searches.
- **Concern:** Launcher market is low-revenue, high-churn. Users rarely switch launchers after finding one.

### Gradient Weather
- **Competition Density:** BLOODBATH — weather is dominated by incumbents (Weather Channel, AccuWeather, Carrot, Windy, Weawow)
- **Top Competitors:** Weawow (free, ad-free), Breezy Weather, Today Weather, Carrot Weather
- **Key Gaps:** Beautiful design but weather is commodity. Users won't pay $3.49/mo for a weather app when free alternatives exist.
- **Concern:** Monetization impossible. Weather apps make money from ads or data — Gradient's privacy stance prevents both.

### LibrePods
- **Competition Density:** LOW — no direct competitors. Unique position.
- **Top Competitors:** None direct. Indirect: MaterialPods (abandoned), CAPod (outdated)
- **Key Gaps:** MASSIVE — millions of AirPods + Android users. First-mover advantage on Play Store.
- **ASO Gaps:** "AirPods on Android" — extremely high intent, low competition.
- **Concern:** Free + open source. No revenue model. Root requirement limits reach.

### Hidgets
- **Competition Density:** MEDIUM — Widgetsmith dominates, many alternatives
- **Top Competitors:** Widgetsmith, Widgetopia, Color Widgets, Pretty Progress
- **Key Gaps:** Health-specific widget focus is a good niche but Widgetsmith can replicate easily.
- **Concern:** Widget apps are one-time $2-5 purchases with zero retention after setup. Not subscription-viable.

### Alpine Lines
- **Competition Density:** LOW-MEDIUM — unique ski resort mechanic, no direct mobile competitors
- **Top Competitors:** Mini Metro (gameplay inspiration), indirect tycoon games
- **Key Gaps:** First ski resort management game on mobile. Unique, charming, free + no ads.
- **Concern:** Free game with no IAP. Developer explicitly made it ad-free. Zero monetization path.

### Sea of Stars
- **Competition Density:** HIGH — RPG market is saturated
- **Top Competitors:** Chrono Trigger, Final Fantasy ports, Stardew Valley mobile
- **Key Gaps:** Already a successful game on PC/console. Mobile port expands reach.
- **Concern:** $9.99 one-time purchase. Already built. Not an opportunity to clone — it's a premium port.

### GLOW (Alarm/NFC)
- **Competition Density:** MEDIUM — Alarmy dominates the "can't-snooze" niche
- **Top Competitors:** Alarmy (50M+ downloads), Sleep Cycle
- **Key Gaps:** NFC tag requirement is more physical than Alarmy's photo/math challenges.
- **Concern:** Alarmy is entrenched. Hardware (NFC tag) requirement adds friction.

## PHASE 4 — SCRUTINY (Winner: BiteSaver — ReciMe Niche)

### Section 1: Market Validation
- Recipe app market growing at 13.4% CAGR, projected $2.5B by 2028.
- Social media cooking content: billions of views monthly across TikTok, Instagram, YouTube.
- Appfigures: global app releases up 60% YoY in Q1 2026. Food & Drink is a growing category.
- ReciMe's App Store presence validates demand — "number 1 recipe organizer" on Play Store.
- WhistleOut picked ReciMe as a top new app for May 2026.

### Section 2: Competition Analysis
- 13+ competitors but clear segmentation: Premium (ReciMe $9.99), Mid (Preplo $4.99), Budget (Paprika $4.99 one-time).
- **Nobody** offers AI auto-generated meal plans from saved recipes.
- **Nobody** offers social cooking challenges (Strava for cooking).
- **Nobody** combines all features at $4.99/mo price point.
- ReciMe's high price ($9.99/mo) is an exploitable vulnerability — 50% undercut is compelling.

### Section 3: Monetization Viability
- Recipe apps command $5-10/mo subscriptions successfully.
- Food = emotional spending. Users will pay for tools that improve their cooking.
- LTV at 6 months: ~$24 net. CAC target: $15 (organic at launch, paid later). Payback: 3 months.
- Annual plan (68% of iOS subscription revenue) at $39.99/yr provides stable base.
- Family plan ($7.99/mo) as viral expansion vector.

### Section 4: Risk Assessment
- **HIGH:** Competition from ReciMe adding AI features. Window is narrow.
- **MEDIUM:** AI extraction quality — must match or beat ReciMe's extraction on day 1.
- **MEDIUM:** Recipe licensing/copyright — extraction from social media sits in grey area (fair use for personal use).
- **LOW:** Technical risk — extraction APIs are commoditized, SwiftUI + Compose are mature.

### Section 5: Timing
- AI integration in recipe apps is accelerating but nobody has "AI meal plans from MY recipes" yet.
- Social cooking is an untapped behavior pattern — first mover advantage.
- App Store algorithm volatility in May 2026 (per ASO World) means new entrants can rank.
- **VERDICT: BUILD**
