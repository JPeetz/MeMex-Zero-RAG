## 3. WEAKNESSES & ATTACK VECTORS

**1. No AI Features:** PokiPaw is entirely manual entry. Every vaccine, every medication dose, every expense must be typed. Our AI scanner turns a photo of a vet bill into structured data in seconds.

**2. No Insurance Integration:** Despite having health records, PokiPaw has zero insurance export features. This is the single biggest gap — insurance claims are the #1 reason pet owners need organized records.

**3. Early-Stage Traction:** <50 reviews means PokiPaw hasn't crossed the social proof threshold. Window of opportunity is open.

**4. Premium Pricing Unknown:** "Coming soon" premium tier means PokiPaw's monetization strategy is undefined. Users may be hit with surprise paywalls later — creates distrust.

**5. No Record Scanning:** All records must be manually entered. A pet with 5 years of vet history = hours of data entry. Our AI scanner makes this a 2-minute photo session.

**6. Small Team:** Solo/small team velocity. Feature development will be slow.

## 4. PRICING COMPARISON

| | PokiPaw | PetVault (Ours) |
|---|---|---|
| Free tier | Unlimited pets, all core features | 1 pet, manual entry, basic reminders |
| Premium | TBD (not launched) | $3.99/mo or $29.99/yr |
| Family sharing | 2 members free | Premium feature |

PokiPaw's free tier is extremely generous — a competitive advantage. However, their free tier lacks AI capabilities (which we offer as Premium). Our free tier is genuinely useful for single-pet owners while Premium unlocks the AI + multi-pet power.

## 5. STRATEGIC POSITIONING

PokiPaw is the closest direct competitor in the "pet health tracker for consumers" space. They've built a solid foundation with breed-specific intelligence, family sharing, and a generous free tier.

**Our counter-positioning:** PokiPaw is a great manual tracker. PetVault is the AI-powered upgrade — photograph your records, get insurance exports, merge multi-vet history automatically. Manual entry vs. AI scan is the conversion wedge.

## 6. VERDICT

**Threat Level:** LOW-MEDIUM (good product, no traction moat, no AI)
**Exploitable Gap:** AI scanning, insurance exports, multi-vet merge
**Action:** Target PokiPaw users who are tired of manual entry. "Your vet handed you 8 pages. With PetVault, that's 30 seconds. With PokiPaw, that's 45 minutes of typing."
