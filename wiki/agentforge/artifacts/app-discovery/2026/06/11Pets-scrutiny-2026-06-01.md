## 3. WEAKNESSES & ATTACK VECTORS

**1. File Upload is Broken:** Multiple App Store reviews explicitly state "file upload does not work at all." For a medical records app, broken document upload is an existential flaw. Pet parents literally cannot store their pet's records in the app.

**2. No Data Visualization:** Users request charts in reviews ("Some data charts would be helpful"). Missing this in a health tracker is a fundamental UX gap. Our scaffold includes weight/vitals trend charts with visual timeline.

**3. No AI Features:** Like all current competitors, 11Pets has zero AI. Manual entry only. Photo of vet bill → structured data is a 10x improvement over manual typing.

**4. Split Focus:** 11Pets is building both consumer and B2B products. This splits engineering resources. Consumer features (like file upload fixes) get deprioritized in favor of business-tier features.

**5. No Insurance Integration:** Despite storing medical records, 11Pets doesn't offer insurance-ready exports. Users must manually compile records when filing claims.

**6. Aging UX:** App Store reviews suggest the interface hasn't seen major modernization. Pet health tracking should feel like Apple Health for pets, not like a database form.

## 5. STRATEGIC POSITIONING

11Pets is the most established "general pet care" app with the longest market presence. Their B2B offering (for groomers, boarders, walkers) gives them revenue diversity. However, their consumer product has stagnated.

**Our counter-positioning:** 11Pets built a platform for the pet care industry. PetVault is built for pet PARENTS. We don't split focus between business and consumer — every engineering hour goes into making the pet parent experience magical.
