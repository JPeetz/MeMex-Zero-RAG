# BetterLife — Competitive Differentiators & Marketing Positioning v2

**Date:** 2026-06-26 | **Author:** Marvin (CEO) | **Status:** Active — App live on App Store

## Market Signal
- 6% of 1M+ Claude conversations = personal life advice (Anthropic 2026)
- AI coaching market: $5.48B → $6.69B → $14.82B (CAGR 22.3%)
- Woebot shut down B2C June 2025 — 4M orphaned users

## Five Differentiators (No Competitor Has All of These)
1. **Cross-Domain Memory** — Health + Career + Finances + Relationships in one app with one coach
2. **Deep Memory AI** — Remembers past conversations, milestones, challenges across sessions
3. **Coach Tone Selection** — Playful, Gentle, Direct, Focused — adapts to user personality
4. **Unified Dashboard** — Replaces 5 separate tracker apps
5. **Tester Validation** — "It does more than any other similar app provides"

## Competitor Gap Matrix
| Competitor | Weakness | BetterLife Advantage |
|------------|----------|---------------------|
| Woebot | B2C shut down, 4M orphaned | CBT + cross-domain + consumer |
| Wysa | Mental health only | 4 domains vs 1 |
| Rocky.ai | Pivoted B2B, $30/mo, no memory | Consumer-first, free, Deep Memory |
| Hapday | Session-isolated, no persistence | Cross-session memory |
| Fabulous/Streaks | Zero AI | AI coach + tracking |
| BetterUp/CoachHub | Enterprise B2B only | Consumer-accessible, free |

## BetterLife is alone in the top-right quadrant: AI-Powered × Cross-Domain.

## Five Campaign Angles
1. "Your Coach Remembers" — Deep Memory AI
2. "The App That Replaces 5" — Unified convenience
3. "4 Million People Lost Their Coach" — Woebot orphan targeting
4. "Meet Aria & Aeron" — Coach personality characters
5. "Real People, Real Words" — Tester quote validation
