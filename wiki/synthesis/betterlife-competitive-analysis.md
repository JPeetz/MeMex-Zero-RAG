## Market Signal
AI life coaching → $7.5B by 2034 (CAGR 15.3%). Woebot shut B2C June 2025, vacating 4M users.

## Competitive Landscape

### Tier 1 — Direct AI Coaches
- **Better Coach** — Single-domain. No multi-domain tracking.
- **Hapday** (4.5★, 18.5K ratings) — Session-isolated voice. No persistent memory.
- **Layers** — Memory from journal only. No biometric/habit/finance data.
- **Rocky.ai** ($30/mo) — Expensive, pure conversation. "Great at asking, not coaching."

### Tier 2 — Habit/Wellbeing
- **Fabulous** (4.7★) — Pre-scripted. No AI. Duke partnership.
- **Streaks** (4.8★, Apple Design Award) — Zero AI. Pure tracking.
- **Finch** (4.8★) — Gamification over transformation.
- **Habitica** (4.5★) — RPG meets habits. Zero AI.

### Tier 3 — Mental Health/CBT
- **Woebot** — SHUT DOWN B2C June 2025. Enterprise-only. Vacated position.
- **Wysa** (4.6★) — Mental health only. $99/mo human coach tier.

## Strategic Gaps (BetterLife's Competitive Moats)
1. Cross-domain memory — no competitor bridges health + career + finances + relationships
2. Unified vs fragmented — one app vs 5 trackers
3. CBT coaching at consumer level — Woebot's 4M orphaned audience
4. Coaching + tracking in one — with persistent memory bridging them
5. "Adaptive Intelligence Across Every Life Domain" — defensible positioning no one owns
