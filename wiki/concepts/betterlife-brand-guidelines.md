# BetterLife.care — Brand Guidelines v1.0

**Created:** 2026-06-26 | **Author:** Marvin (CEO) | **Canonical:** `~/workspace/brand/BetterLife-brand-guidelines.md`

## Brand Identity
- **Name:** BetterLife | **Domain:** betterlife.care | **Tagline:** Growth that adapts to you.
- **Product:** AI-powered life coaching app — two coach personas: Aria (empathetic), Aeron (direct)
- **Mood:** Premium, polished, futuristic, calm luxury, aspirational but warm

## Logo
Eight-point compass star — faceted, crystalline, glowing neon magenta/violet on deep navy background.
- **File:** `betterlife-app-icon.png`
- **Rules:** Never modify, stretch, or place on light backgrounds. Always maintain clear space.

### FORBIDDEN
- No white backgrounds. No bright greens, yellows, oranges. No pastels. No new primaries without Board approval.

## Typography
- **Headings:** Space Grotesk Bold (600-700)
- **Body:** Space Grotesk Regular/Medium (400-500)
- **Mono:** Fira Code
- **Never:** serif fonts, novelty fonts

## Imagery
- Photographic: Dark, moody, atmospheric, low-key lighting, neon accents
- Illustration: Faceted geometric, gradient overlays, compass/star motifs, glassmorphism
- Video: 9:16, dark backgrounds, particles, Space Grotesk titles, vignette

## Brand Enforcement
1. BEFORE generating any image/video: consult brand guidelines
2. AFTER generating: self-audit against forbidden colors
3. If in doubt: dark background + Space Grotesk + brand blue = safe fallback
4. Marketing content MUST pass brand audit before publishing
5. New color additions require Board approval
