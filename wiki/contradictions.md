# Contradictions

> Pending conflicts that need human resolution. The LLM flags them here; YOU decide truth.

---

## How This Works

1. During ingest, the LLM detects when new information conflicts with existing wiki content
2. It adds an entry here with both claims and their sources
3. It STOPS and asks you to decide
4. You resolve by choosing which claim is authoritative (or that both are valid)
5. The LLM updates the wiki and marks the entry resolved
